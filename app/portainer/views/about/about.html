About lighterDOCK & lighter-Portainer
lighterDOCK is a free and open-source software based on Docker brought to you with by Constantine3 and Saafo.
lighter-Portainer is a free and open-source software based on Portainer brought to you with by Saafo and Constantine3.
Portainer is a free and open-source software brought to you with by portainer.io and contributors.
lighter-Portainer has full support for lighterDOCK and partial support for Docker (some features may not be available, like registry or pull).
